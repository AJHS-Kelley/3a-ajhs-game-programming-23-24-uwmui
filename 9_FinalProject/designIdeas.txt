Game Type: Pirate Themed RPG/Dungeon Crawler.

Levels: Beach (with dock leading to a ship that takes you to the 2nd level), enemy ship interior.

Enemies / Obstacles: Enemy pirates (ranged and melee), skeletons (melee only), pit fall traps, quicksand traps, trapped chests disguised as real chests, etc.

Equipment: Cutlass, armor, possibly ranged options.

Loot: Gold, better equipment, healing items.

Win Condition: Defeating the boss at the end of the ship.

Loss Condition: Reaching 0 health

Difficulties: Safe, Normal
    Safe: More health, less aggressive enemies, fewer enemies, lower, enemy health, etc.
    Normal: normal
